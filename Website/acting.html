Acting

When I was three days old, my parents escaped my siblings and brought me to the movies to watch Tom Stoppard's Shakespeare In Love. While I doubt I remembered any of that film, fourteen years later I would act in my first Shakespeare scene and four years after that my first Stoppard scene. My love of Acting stems from a passion to storytell and to hopefully inspire awe in the process.
